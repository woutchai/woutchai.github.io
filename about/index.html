About Mocha Chai

Mocha Chai pairs two beloved comforts: cocoa and masala chai. It blends the round, bittersweet depth of chocolate with black tea simmered in milk and spices like cardamom, cinnamon, ginger, and clove.

Origins

While traditional chai has roots across the Indian subcontinent, the “mocha” addition is a modern café adaptation. It borrows the chocolate note familiar from espresso mochas and marries it to chai’s warming spice profile.

Ingredients at a Glance

  • Loose black tea (Assam or CTC)
  • Milk of choice (dairy or plant‑based)
  • Fresh or ground spices (cardamom, cinnamon, ginger, clove)
  • Unsweetened cocoa or cacao powder
  • Sweetener to taste

Flavor

Expect a velvety cup with spice up front, cocoa rounding the middle, and a gentle tea finish. Adjust sweetness and spice intensity to your mood.
